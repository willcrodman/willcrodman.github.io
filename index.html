Introduction

As a passionate individual pursuing a major in Mathematics, Computer Science, and a minor in Economics at Tulane University, I am driven to leverage my expertise in the field of data science. My experiences in technology consulting, university research, and assistant teaching, has honed my skills in scrum methodology, data analytics, and leadership.

I am confident that my technical skill set, coupled with my commitment to professional growth, positions me for a promising career in data science.
